Make your blog private? Block everyone or even registered users. Or a selection of them... Need a maintenance mode? Or just want the site to youself.. AccessQontrol can do it all!

== Description ==

You can allow just a selected group on your site. AccessQontrol is the plugin to do it. Clean and simple. Using the simple options panel you can shield your blog from anyone or just allow a selected group of users.

**Features**

* Easy installation
* Make your site for registered users only
* Block everyone but yourself
* Create an exception list of people not to block
* Shows a nice template telling people they're not welcome (or whatever you put there)
* Easy administration

== Installation ==

Unzip the file and upload it to your /wp-content/plugins/ folder.<br />
Activate from the plugins page and administrate via the options panel.

[More instructions](http://meandmymac.net/contact-and-support/accessqontrol/#install)
